About ADAPT

ADAPT (Alzheimer Disease Analysis and Prediction Tool) is a cutting-edge AI system designed to assist healthcare professionals in the early detection and accurate staging of Alzheimer’s disease.

Using advanced deep learning algorithms trained on thousands of brain scans, ADAPT can classify brain images into different stages of Alzheimer’s disease with high accuracy, providing valuable diagnostic support for clinicians.

Our tool aims to improve early intervention, treatment planning, and patient outcomes through precise classification and intuitive visualization of results.

Key Features

Advanced Classification

Accurately classifies brain scans into different stages using state-of-the-art deep learning models.

Detailed Metrics

Provides accuracy metrics and confidence scores to support clinical decision-making.

Visual Explanations

Utilizes GradCAM technology to highlight regions of interest in brain scans.

How It Works

  1. Upload Brain Scan
    Upload MRI or CT scan images. The system accepts standard formats.
  2. AI Analysis
    Our deep learning model analyzes the image, identifying patterns associated with Alzheimer’s.
  3. Results & Visualization
    Get accuracy metrics and GradCAM visualizations of affected regions.
